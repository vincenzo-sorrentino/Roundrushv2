## Objective
Provide a timeline-oriented planning view where teams can visualise epic and module delivery schedules, milestones, and release targets.

## In scope
- Timeline / Gantt-style roadmap visualisation
- Milestone tracking and release planning
- Epic-level scheduling and sequencing

## Out of scope (for this EP)
- Resource allocation and capacity planning
- Financial budgeting or cost tracking
- External stakeholder portal
